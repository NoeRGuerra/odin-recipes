Air Fried Chicken

Description

These air fryer chicken tenderloins beat chicken nuggets any day and the recipe is so simple. I tend to use chicken tenderloins but you can use sliced chicken breasts if easier.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • Â½ cup dry bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 chicken tenderloins

Preparation

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Overhead of crumbled chicken tenderloin ingredients in various bowls and measuring cups.
  3. Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Whisk egg in a small bowl.
  5. Overhead of whisked eggs in a bowl.
  6. Mix bread crumbs and oil together in a second bowl until mixture becomes loose and crumbly.
  7. Dip each chicken tenderloin into the bowl of egg; shake off any residual egg. Dip chicken into crumb mixture, making sure it is evenly and fully covered.
  8. Overhead of raw chicken breasts being dipped in an egg mixture, breaded, and placed on a baking sheet.
  9. Lay chicken tenderloins in the air fryer basket in an even layer.
  10. Air-fry until no longer pink in the center, about 12 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  11. Overhead of cooked breaded chicken tenderloins in an air fryer basket.
  12. Serve hot and enjoy!
